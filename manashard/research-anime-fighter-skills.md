### SWORD ART ONLINE - The Gold Standard for Weapon Skill Systems

**System Mechanic:** Sword Skills are activated via **Pre-Motion** (a specific starting pose). The system recognizes the motion, the weapon glows, and the system auto-completes the attack at superhuman speed. After completion, the user enters **Post-Motion** (a vulnerability window). Skills have **Cooldown** timers.

**Why This is Peak Fighter Fantasy:** No magic. No divine power. Just weapon technique executed through the game system. The skill system literally rewards mastering sword forms.

#### One-Handed Sword Skills (The Fighter's Bread & Butter)

| Skill Name | Hits | Description | Fighter Archetype Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Slant** | 1 | A diagonal slash. The most basic attack. | Foundation technique - the first thing a fighter learns |
| **Horizontal** | 1 | A horizontal slash | Basic lateral cut |
| **Vertical** | 1 | A vertical slash | Basic overhead/downward cut |
| **Uppercut** | 1 | An upward slash from low guard | Rising strike from below |
| **Rage Spike** | 1 | A charging leap followed by an upward strike | Gap-closer/engage skill |
| **Sonic Leap** | 1 | Charges then dashes at enemy with a downward slash | Aggressive gap-closing dash attack |
| **Vorpal Strike** | 1 | Extremely forceful single thrust with range equal to 2x blade length. Hits as hard as a two-handed polearm. | The fighter's "big hit" - raw power in one thrust |
| **Snake Bite** | 2 | Two rapid swings (left-right, right-left). Targets the opponent's weapon and can break it. | Disarming technique - tactical fighter move |
| **Horizontal Arc** | 3 | Left-right swing, right-left swing, then one more powerful left-right swing | Triple-slash combo |
| **Sharp Nail** | 3 | Upward diagonal slash, horizontal slash, downward diagonal slash | Three-direction combo |
| **Savage Fulcrum** | 3 | Right-left horizontal slash, then 90-degree rotation into bottom-up vertical slash, finishing with full-power top-down vertical. Traces the number "4" in the air. | Complex weapon art with directional changes |
| **Horizontal Square** | 4 | Four horizontal slashes in alternating directions with body spin momentum | Whirlwind-style horizontal combo |
| **Vertical Square** | 4 | Overhead vertical slash, two upward verticals, concluding full-power downward slash | Vertical chain combo |
| **Howling Octave** | 8 | Five high-speed thrusts followed by downward cut, upward cut, finishing with full-force upward cut. 40% Physical, 60% Fire. | Rapid-thrust combo with elemental overlay |
| **Meteor Break** | 7 | Downward strike right, reverse swing up, LEFT SHOULDER RAM, vertical swing, RIGHT SHOULDER RAM, horizontal neck slash. Combines Martial Arts + Sword. | Mixed martial arts + swordsmanship combo - peak fighter |

#### Dual Wield Skills (Kirito's Unique)

| Skill Name | Hits | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Starburst Stream** | 16 | 16-hit rapid dual-sword combo. Each hit gains momentum and becomes more devastating. |
| **The Eclipse** | 27 | Ultimate dual-wield technique. 27-hit combo. The most powerful player skill in SAO. |

**Key Fighter Concepts from SAO:**
- **Original Sword Skills (OSS):** Players who master combat can CREATE their own sword skills by combining movements they've practiced extensively. Pure skill expression.
- **Switch:** A party technique where fighters trade positions mid-combo to chain attacks during each other's Post-Motion windows. Tactical team fighting.
- **Skill Connect:** Chaining different Sword Skills together by canceling Post-Motion into a new Pre-Motion. Advanced combo technique.

### OVERLORD - The Martial Arts System

**System Mechanic:** Martial Arts are skills cultivated through intense training. They did NOT exist in the game YGGDRASIL - they are unique to the New World's warriors. Ainz calls them "the magic of warriors." Users can only maintain a limited number of active Martial Arts simultaneously (typically 5-7). **Limit Breaker** allows exceeding this cap at a cost.

**Why This is Peak Fighter Fantasy:** Overlord's martial arts are explicitly non-magical abilities that warriors develop through training. They are the warrior's answer to magic.

### LOG HORIZON - MMORPG Warrior Classes

**System Mechanic:** Elder Tale MMORPG. Skills are either Auto-Unlock (at level thresholds) or Optional (purchased with proficiency points). Most skills have cooldowns. Warrior classes are the Tank role.

#### Samurai Class (DPS-Tank Hybrid - Most "Fighter" of the three)

|---|---|---|

## 5. SYNTHESIS: CORE FIGHTER SKILL ARCHETYPES

Based on all research, fighter skills across anime/manga/manhwa fall into these categories:

### A. BASIC WEAPON ARTS
Single-hit to multi-hit weapon techniques with increasing complexity. (SAO Sword Skills, Demon Slayer Forms, Zoro's Santoryu)

### B. PHYSICAL ENHANCEMENT
Self-buff skills that increase stats temporarily. (Overlord's Body Strengthening/Flow Acceleration, Nen's Ren/Ko, Cursed Energy Reinforcement)

### C. STANCE/STYLE SWITCHING
Changing between offensive and defensive fighting modes. (Mushoku Tensei's three styles, Benimaru's Sword of Motion/Stillness, Dead Calm)

### D. COMBAT PERCEPTION
Battle awareness skills that read opponents. (Overlord's Vital Spot Detection, Grimgar's The Line, Sasaki's Thousand Image Defense, Soujirou's Clairvoyance)

### E. COMBO/CHAIN SYSTEMS
Skills that reward sequential hits with escalating damage. (SAO's Skill Connect, Overgeared's Sword Dance combos, Bell's Chain Attack, Shangri-La's Combo Skills)

### F. COUNTER/PARRY TECHNIQUES
Defensive skills that punish attackers. (Full Counter, Water God Style's Flow, Overlord's Fortress/Instant Counter, Dead Calm)

### G. MOVEMENT/POSITIONING
Gap closers, evasion, and mobility skills. (Soru, Sprint, Burst Step, Flow Acceleration, Geppo)

### H. ULTIMATE/LIMIT BREAK
All-or-nothing devastating attacks with high cost. (Final Getsuga Tensho, Kill, Eclipse, Kaio-ken x20, Berserker Armor, Revenge Counter)

### I. BATTLE AURA/KILLING INTENT
Intimidation and presence skills. (Bloodlust, King's Force, War Cry, Kenpachi's Reiatsu)

### J. WEAPON ENHANCEMENT
Infusing weapons with energy/aura without being "magic." (Aura Slash, Shu, Cutting Edge, Getsuga Tensho)
