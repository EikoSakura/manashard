### REINCARNATED AS A SWORD (Tensei shitara Ken deshita)

**System Mechanic:** Skills are acquired through level-ups, skill points, and absorption via the sword (Teacher). Skills have numbered proficiency levels (e.g., Fire Magic 4, Stealth 4). The sword can share its skills with its wielder Fran. Class system includes Spellsword.

#### Unique Mechanics for Tactical RPG

- **Skill Sharing via Weapon:** The sword shares its skill pool with Fran. In a tactical RPG, a "Sentient Weapon" could grant bonus skills to its wielder based on bond level.
- **Awakening:** A transformation that dramatically boosts stats (especially speed) and grants new elemental attacks. Could translate as a once-per-battle "Awakening" state with stat buffs and new skill access.
- **Killer Titles** (Undead Killer, Fiend Killer, etc.): Passive damage bonuses against specific enemy types. Translates directly to racial effectiveness bonuses.

#### Unique Mechanics for Tactical RPG

- **Sub-Skill Architecture:** Each major skill contains multiple sub-abilities. A single "Predator" skill could unlock Predation, Analysis, Mimicry as the skill levels up.

### SHANGRI-LA FRONTIER

**System Mechanic:** Skills level up through use and have proficiency levels. Skills can be combined at the **Skill Garden** facility to create **Combo Skills** based on job and sub-job. Characters have a main job and sub-job. Weapons/skills can have special attributes like **Crush** (destroys body parts, leaving weak points).

#### Mage-Type Abilities

| Skill Name | Type | Description | Mage Archetype Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Elemental Magic** (various) | Job Skill | Element-based spells that scale with proficiency level | Standard elemental casting |
| **Material Focus** | Skill | Channels power into physical materials for enhanced effects | Enchantment casting |
| **Glowing Pierce** | Combo Skill | Energy-infused piercing attack | Spellblade technique |

#### Thief-Type Abilities

| Skill Name | Type | Description | Thief Archetype Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Assassin Pierce** | Combo Skill | Boosts attack force when the enemy hasn't noticed you OR when their attack focus is elsewhere | Backstab / flanking bonus |
| **Infight** | Combo Skill | Adds extra melee damage at extreme close range | Point-blank burst |
| **Accel** (Lv MAX) | Base Skill | Movement speed acceleration | Sprint / dash |
| **Fighting Spirit** (Lv 7) | Base Skill | Boosts combat intensity | Attack power buff |
| **Climax Boost** | Combo: Accel + Fighting Spirit | Massive temporary stat increase combining speed and power | Ultimate burst mode |
| **Airgetlam** | Combo: Hand of Fortune + Shock Fist | Grants bonus damage for critical hits based on Luck stat | Lucky critical amplifier |
| **Critical Assist** (Lv 3) | Base Skill | Increases critical hit rate | Crit chance passive |
| **Knockout** | Combo: Power Direct + Critical Assist | Chance of instant KO effect on critical hits | Critical finisher |

#### Priest/Support Abilities

| Skill Name | Type | Description | Priest Archetype Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fortress Breaker** | Combo Skill (Bilac) | Breaks through enemy defensive formations | Barrier piercing support |

#### Unique Mechanics for Tactical RPG

- **Skill Garden / Combo Skills:** Two or more leveled skills can be fused into a more powerful combo skill. This is an excellent tactical RPG mechanic - players invest in base skills to unlock powerful combos.
- **Crush Attribute:** Attacks with Crush destroy a body part and leave a **weak point**. Subsequent attacks on the weak point deal bonus damage. Grid-based RPG translation: mark a tile/enemy with "Crush" status, allies attacking that target get bonus damage.
- **Proficiency-Gated Evolution:** Skills only combine when they reach required proficiency thresholds. Rewards dedicated use of specific skills.
- **Job + Sub-Job Combos:** The combo skills available depend on your job combination. Different class combos unlock different fusion options.

- **Purchased Skills:** Skills cost eiress and must be practiced. Creates an economic decision - save money for better skills or buy cheap ones now?
- **The Line (Thief):** A perception-based deathblow mechanic. Could translate as: when a Thief has line-of-sight to an enemy's back for 2+ turns, they can attempt a guaranteed critical.
- **Priest as Hybrid:** Grimgar priests fight AND heal. They wear armor and melee. Directly supports a Fire Emblem-style Priest who isn't helpless.

## 4. SYNTHESIS: CORE SKILL ARCHETYPES BY CLASS

Based on all research, skills across anime/manga/manhwa fall into these categories per class:

---

### MAGE SKILL ARCHETYPES

#### A. ELEMENTAL DAMAGE SPELLS
Core offensive magic organized by element with scaling tiers. (Overlord Tier Spells, Tensura Elemental Magic, SAO ALO racial magic, Reincarnated Sword Fire/Wind Magic)

#### B. CROWD CONTROL / STATUS MAGIC
Spells that disable, slow, sleep, root, or stun enemies. (Astral Hypnos, Grasp Heart stun, Sleepy Shadow, Temporal Stasis, Shadow Bond)

#### C. BUFF/DEBUFF CASTING
Spells that enhance allies or weaken enemies without doing direct damage. (Keen Edge, Haste, Thorn Bind Hostage, Mind Shock, Elixir, Bloodlust)

#### D. META-MAGIC / SPELL AMPLIFICATION
Modifiers that enhance other spells. (Maximize Magic, Twin Maximize, Great Sage optimization, Concurrent Chanting, Argonaut charging)

#### E. SPATIAL / UTILITY MAGIC
Teleportation, flight, detection, and utility spells. (Blink, Spatial Motion, Fly, Shadow Exchange, Phantom Body)

#### F. INSTANT DEATH / HIGH-TIER
Supreme-tier spells with dramatic effects. (Grasp Heart, True Death, Vermilion Nova, Holy-Demonic Magic)

#### G. ANALYTICAL / TACTICAL
Information-gathering and combat prediction. (Great Sage, Analytical Appraisal, Mana Perception, Full Control Encounter)

---

### THIEF SKILL ARCHETYPES

#### A. STEALTH / INVISIBILITY
Hiding from detection - from basic concealment to complete presence erasure. (Stealth, Perfect Unknowable, Shadow Walk, Hiding, Shadow Lurk)

#### B. ASSASSINATION / BURST DAMAGE
Single massive-damage strikes, often with conditions (from behind, unseen, etc.). (Assassinate, Backstab, Assassin Pierce, Mutilate, The Line/deathblow)

#### C. SPEED / MOBILITY
Movement enhancement, dashing, teleportation, and repositioning. (Sprint, Blink, Accel, Trick Step, Soru-equivalent)

#### D. POISONING / DAMAGE-OVER-TIME
Applying ongoing damage through poison, bleed, or curses. (Death Stinger, Poison Attack)

#### E. PERCEPTION / DETECTION
Finding traps, hidden enemies, and enemy weak points. (Presence Sense, Searching, Tracking, Dark Vision, Night Vision, Vital Spot Detection)

#### F. CRITICAL HIT ENHANCEMENT
Skills that increase critical rate, critical damage, or add effects to criticals. (Critical Assist, Knockout, Airgetlam luck-based crit bonus, The Line)

#### G. CLONE / MISDIRECTION
Creating decoys, illusions, and diversions. (Shadow Lurk clones, Lord Mirage, Phantom Body, Mimicry)

#### H. LOOT / UTILITY
Non-combat thief specialties. (Picking, Expert Carver/Disassembly, Trap Detection)

---

### PRIEST SKILL ARCHETYPES

#### A. DIRECT HEALING
HP restoration spells of varying speed and efficiency. (Heal, Healing Light, Cure, Healing Magic, Recovery Magic)

#### B. STATUS CLEANSING
Removing debuffs, poison, curses, and negative effects. (Cure status, Dispel, Holy Magic purification)

#### C. REACTIVE / PREEMPTIVE HEALING
Heals that trigger automatically when damage is taken. (Log Horizon Reactive Healing, Multilayer Barrier)

#### D. RESURRECTION / DEATH PROTECTION
Bringing back fallen allies or preventing death. (Resurrection, Perfect Memory EX, Endless Regeneration EX)

#### E. BUFF / ENHANCEMENT
Strengthening allies' stats, damage, or defenses. (Protection, Vanadis Tevere, Keen Edge via support, Elixir healing amplification)

#### F. BARRIER / SHIELD MAGIC
Protective fields that absorb or negate damage. (Multilayer Barrier, Wall of Protection)

#### G. HOLY DAMAGE
Offensive priest spells, often effective against undead/demons. (Smite, Holy Magic, Holy-Demonic damage)

#### H. AURA / FIELD EFFECTS
Persistent area effects that heal or protect allies in range. (Monarch's Domain, Reactive Healing fields)

## 6. CROSS-CLASS UNIQUE MECHANICS FOR TACTICAL RPG

These mechanics are inspired by the anime research and would add strategic depth to a grid-based system:

| Mechanic Name | Source Inspiration | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Combo Skills** | Shangri-La Frontier Skill Garden | Two base skills at sufficient level can be fused into a stronger combo skill. E.g., "Ember Lance Lv5" + "Chain Bolt Lv3" = "Storm Flare" (fire+lightning AoE) |
| **Skill Evolution** | Solo Leveling, Tensura | Skills can evolve into advanced versions at max level. E.g., "Venom Edge" Lv10 evolves into "Plague Fang" with AoE poison |
| **Awakening** | Reincarnated as a Sword | Once per battle, a unit can Awaken, gaining a 3-turn stat boost and access to a unique Awakening skill. Requires bond/trust threshold. |
| **Reactive Triggers** | Log Horizon Reactive Healing | Some skills are "pre-cast" and trigger when conditions are met (ally takes damage, enemy enters range, etc.). Adds planning depth. |
| **Spell Modifiers** | Overlord Maximize/Twin Magic | Spend extra MP to modify any spell: Maximize (guaranteed max damage), Widen (larger AoE), Quicken (reduced cast time) |
| **Mark and Execute** | Log Horizon Thorn Bind Hostage, Vital Sight | Debuff skills that "mark" enemies. Marked enemies take bonus damage from allies, enabling team coordination. |
| **Crush / Weak Point** | Shangri-La Frontier Crush | Certain attacks create weak points on enemies. Subsequent attacks targeting the weak point deal bonus damage and can ignore armor. |
| **Stealth + Flanking** | Solo Leveling, Log Horizon Assassin | Thieves gain damage bonuses for attacking from specific positions (behind, sides). Stealth allows repositioning to achieve these positions. |
| **Charged Casting** | Danmachi Argonaut | Skip a turn to "charge" the next spell. Charged spells deal double damage, increased AoE, or gain bonus effects. |
| **Overskills** | Log Horizon | When two skill trees are both at Lv7+, a special hybrid skill becomes available. Rewards mastering multiple skill lines. |
